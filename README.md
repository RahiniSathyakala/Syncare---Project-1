# 🚑 Syncare – AI-Powered Patient Monitoring System

> **No patient unseen. No life lost due to delay.**

Syncare is an AI-powered patient monitoring system prototype designed to provide affordable, intelligent, and continuous healthcare monitoring for patients receiving long-term care at home.

The system continuously analyzes vital signs, predicts potential emergencies, simulates real-time alert workflows, and demonstrates how technology can improve response time during critical situations.

---

## 🌟 Problem Statement

In India, prolonged hospitalization is often financially unsustainable for low- and middle-income families. Critically ill patients requiring continuous observation are frequently discharged home, where sudden deterioration can go unnoticed.

Family members cannot monitor patients 24/7, and delayed recognition of emergencies can result in severe complications or even loss of life.

Syncare was conceptualized to bridge this gap by bringing **hospital-like vigilance into the home environment**.

---

## 💡 Our Solution

Syncare is an AI-driven remote patient monitoring prototype that:

- Continuously tracks patient vitals,
- Detects abnormal physiological patterns,
- Predicts potential emergencies,
- Simulates emergency escalation workflows,
- Guides caregivers with first-aid instructions,
- Generates incident reports for clinicians and families.

> **Our mission is simple: to ensure timely intervention when every second matters.**

---

## ✨ Features

### 📊 Real-Time Patient Dashboard
Monitor simulated patient vitals including:

- ❤️ Heart Rate
- 🫁 SpO₂
- 🩸 Blood Pressure
- 🌡 Temperature
- 💨 Respiratory Rate

---

### 🚨 Emergency Simulation Engine

Simulate critical healthcare scenarios such as:

- Cardiac Emergency
- Respiratory Distress
- Sepsis
- Recovery Mode

---

### 🧠 AI Health Stability Score

An AI-inspired scoring mechanism that dynamically reflects the patient's current condition.

---

### 🔮 Early Warning Predictions

Demonstrates predictive healthcare by forecasting deterioration trends before emergencies occur.

Example:

> *"SpO₂ decreasing steadily. Estimated deterioration in 5 minutes."*

---

### 📞 Emergency Alert Workflow

Simulates real-world escalation pathways by notifying:

- 🏥 Hospitals
- 🚑 Ambulance Services
- 👩‍⚕️ Nursing Teams
- 👨‍👩‍👧 Family Members

---

### 🩹 First Aid Guidance

Provides immediate caregiver instructions while professional help is on the way.

---

### 📄 Incident Report Generator

Automatically generates structured emergency reports containing:

- Event details
- Vital summaries
- Probable causes
- Recommended interventions

---

### 👨‍👩‍👧 Family Dashboard

Displays:

- Stability trends
- Alert history
- Emergency summaries
- Overall patient wellness indicators

---

### 📶 Offline Edge AI Simulation

Demonstrates how healthcare monitoring could continue in low-connectivity environments through local processing and queued alerts.

## ⚠️ Disclaimer

This project is an **educational proof-of-concept prototype** developed for innovation, learning, and demonstration purposes.

- All patient data displayed are **simulated**.
- The system is **not intended for clinical use**.
- Deployment in healthcare environments would require:
